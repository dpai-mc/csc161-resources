Assignment: Week 1 Lab

VPN Connectivity & Environment Setup

Objective

The primary goal of this assignment is to successfully establish a secure connection to the course lab environment using the OpenVPN client and the provided configuration files. You will be guided through this process by an AI Tutor, "Dr. Anya Sharma." This initial connection is the foundational step required for all future hands-on labs.

Background

In the real world, IT professionals rarely work in a vacuum. They follow established procedures and often use guided assistance or documentation to perform complex tasks. This assignment simulates that experience. You will interact with an AI Tutor designed to act as an expert guide. Your task is to follow its instructions precisely to install, configure, and verify your connection to our private lab network.

Instructions

  1. Gather Your Materials: Before you begin, ensure you have downloaded the following two files from the course's Canvas page:
    • The OpenVPN configuration file (ending in `.ovpn`).
    • Your personal credential file named `secret`.
  2. Engage the AI Tutor: Start a new session with the AI Tutor for this assignment. The tutor will introduce herself as "Dr. Anya Sharma." Follow her lead.
  3. Follow the Guided Process: Dr. Sharma will guide you step-by-step through downloading and installing the client, importing the profile, authenticating, and verifying your connection.
  4. Capture Your Proof of Connection: Once the AI Tutor guides you to run the `ping 10.255.254.1` command and you see successful replies, take a screenshot of the entire command prompt or terminal window.
  5. Document Your Interaction: Copy the entire transcript of your conversation with Dr. Sharma and paste this into a plain text file.

Student Deliverables

You will submit two files to the Canvas assignment portal:

  • A single screenshot (`.png` or `.jpg`) of your terminal or command prompt showing the successful `ping` to `10.255.254.1`.
  • A text file (`.txt`) containing the complete, unedited transcript of your interaction with the AI Tutor, Dr. Anya Sharma.
