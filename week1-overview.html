Week 1 Activity

Lab Environment Setup & Connectivity

Welcome to CSC 161! The goal of this first activity is to ensure every student can successfully connect to our class's virtual lab environment. This involves installing a VPN client, establishing a secure connection, and verifying that you can access your personal nested Proxmox VE instance. Completing these steps is essential for all future labs in this course.

  • Understand the purpose of a Virtual Private Network (VPN) for creating a secure, encrypted connection over a public network, using OpenVPN as a practical example.
  • Define what a hypervisor is and differentiate between Type 1 (bare-metal) and Type 2 (hosted) hypervisors.
  • Log into and identify key components of a Type 1 hypervisor's web interface (Proxmox VE).
  • Apply basic network troubleshooting commands to verify connectivity within a private network.
  • Synthesize initial environment details to prepare for a guided lab with an AI assistant.
  • A modern computer (Windows, macOS, or Linux).
  • Your assigned lab credentials (username/password).
  • Your assigned Proxmox VE IP address.
  • The `CSC161-Fall2025.ovpn` configuration file.

Activity Procedure

You will connect to a **nested Proxmox VE instance**, your own personal virtual server. This gives you full admin control without affecting other students.

Our Network: 10.255.254.0/24

Gateway: 10.255.254.1

Download and install the OpenVPN Connect client from the official website. Follow the installer prompts, accepting the default settings.

Download OpenVPN Client

Launch OpenVPN Connect, go to the "File" tab, and import the `CSC161-Fall2025.ovpn` file. Enter your lab credentials when prompted and click "Connect".

Open a command prompt or terminal and ping the lab gateway. You should see successful replies.

ping 10.255.254.1

In a web browser, navigate to `https://[Your-IP]:8006`. Accept the security warning for the self-signed certificate and log in with your lab credentials.

https://10.255.254.101:8006

Congratulations! You are now connected and ready to begin the first guided lab. The next step is to interact with the **AI Tutor "Alex"**. Be prepared to provide your student VM name and assigned IP address.
